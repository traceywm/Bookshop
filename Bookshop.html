Suggested Reading


Siku Njema

By Ken Walibora

£10.99

Siku Njema is a popular Swahili novel written by the renowned Kenyan author, Ken Walibora.
The novel was published in 1996 and saw Walibora become an instant household name in Swahili fiction.

An amazing novel, focusing on the countries at the forefront of liberation.
